CARS WE HAVE
MARUTHI SUZUKI

Among all the top car brands in India, Maruti Suzuki is the king. Having a diverse portfolio, Maruti Suzuki makes

affordable hatchbacks, premium sedans, compact SUVs and LUVs. The company is a leader in the A and B segments,

with popular selling models such as the Ertiga MPV and Vitara Brezza compact SUV. The company also has a driving

school and dealership network across the country.

TATA MOTORS

The new model targeted the mass market, solved quality issues and brought in global suppliers. Last year,

the Nexon was Tata Motors' best-selling vehicle. But it wasn't always like that. The company is still undergoing some

changes, and Nexon is an excellent example. Despite the company's high competition, it remains a great

place to buy a car. Whether you're buying a car for yourself or a family member,

Tata Motors is a name you can trust.

HUNDAI

Of the top car companies on India's list, Hyundai is the third choice. The Korean

car manufacturer Hyundai has two production facilities in India. One is in Sriperumbudur, while the other is in

Irungattukottai. Together, these facilities can produce 9 lakh cars a year. With these manufacturing facilities,

Hyundai is one of the largest car manufacturers in the country. In India, Hyundai offers eleven models.

KIA

The company has three models in its Indian lineup and is currently ranked fourth in

the list of best-selling car brands for March 2021. The Carnival and Seltos continue to do well in themarket,

while the Sonet and Carens are decent sellers. The company is planning to launch a seven-seater Sonet. This car is

capable of tackling the competition from other luxury brands.
